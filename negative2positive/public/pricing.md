# Pricing — Negative Converter

Negative Converter is **free, with no paid tiers, no account, and no usage limits**.

## Free

- **Price**: $0 / forever
- **Account**: Not required
- **Image upload limit**: None — all processing runs locally in your browser
- **Output limit**: Unlimited
- **Watermark**: None
- **Features**:
  - Color, B&W, and positive (slide) film modes
  - All 37 film presets (Kodak, Fujifilm, Ilford, CineStill, etc.)
  - RAW support: CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG (including iPhone ProRaw), RW2
  - 16-bit PNG and TIFF export
  - Auto frame detection, auto rotation, one-click white balance
  - Optional lens correction (Lensfun profiles)
  - Batch processing with roll-level settings
  - Offline desktop apps (Windows, macOS, Linux)

## Why free

The project is open source under the MIT license. There is no server-side image processing, so there is no per-user cost to operate. Source: <https://github.com/lexluthor0304/NegativeConverter>.
